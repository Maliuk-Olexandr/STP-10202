About the Game

You Were Born for Magic

Magicabin: Witch’s Adventure is a spellbinding match-3 puzzle game wrapped in a charming, magical narrative. Play as a young witch reclaiming her ancestral cabin and unlocking forgotten spells across enchanted realms. Gather ingredients, restore harmony, and discover the secrets of the woods.

How to Play

Simple to Learn, Deeply Magical to Master

  • Match 3 or more magical items (crystals, herbs, potions) 1

  • Match 4+ to craft powerful spells and magical bursts 2

  • Combine spells for wild chain reactions 3

  • Fulfill quests and collect rare artifacts to unlock new realms 4

Every match uncovers part of your journey — from humble witch to guardian of the forest!

Magical Combos

Wield the Power of the Ancients

Harness combo magic through smart matching:

  • Moonstone Orb

    Clears an entire row or column

  • Enchanted Vial

  • Celestial Sigil

    Erases all items of one kind

  • Mystic Fusion

Every match is a ritual—use them wisely!
